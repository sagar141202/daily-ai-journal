**Imagining Algorithms**

Imagine you're on a treasure hunt, but instead of gold coins, you're searching for a way to make your computer do a task over and over again without getting stuck in an endless loop. This is where algorithms come in – a set of instructions, like a treasure map, that guides the computer through a series of steps to achieve a goal. Just like how the map leads you to the treasure, an algorithm helps the computer find the solution to a problem. With each step, the computer gets closer to finding that elusive "treasure" – the answer to the problem.
