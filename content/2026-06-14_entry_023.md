**Unleash the Power of `watch`**

Imagine you're a master chef, carefully monitoring the temperature of a sauce that's about to boil over. Just as you need to stay vigilant, `watch` is the watchful eye you need on your system. This command repeatedly runs a specified command, updating the output in real-time, allowing you to track file changes, system events, or even the progress of a long-running job.

**Watch in Action**

Ever felt like you're stuck waiting for a long process to finish, wondering if it's ever going to complete? `watch --interval 2 sleep 10` is your answer. This command will run in the background, pausing for 10 seconds at a time, and update its output every 2 seconds, giving you the perfect snapshot of time as the clock ticks away.
