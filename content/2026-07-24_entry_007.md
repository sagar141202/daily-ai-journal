**Discover the Power of 'screen'**

Imagine yourself at your Linux terminal, coding a complex algorithm on a 10-hour deadline. As you take a crucial phone call, the process freezes, and your mind goes haywire. That's where the `screen` command comes to the rescue - a secret agent that clones your terminal session, shielding it from any potential interruptions. With a simple `Ctrl + A` followed by `D`, your process continues to run in the background, allowing you to multitask like a pro.

**Unleash the Force of 'strace'**

Are you a seasoned Linux user struggling to debug a pesky process? Look no further than the 'strace' command - a master of unraveling the mystery behind a program's every step. By wrapping your application in a thread of diagnostic data, strace reveals a treasure map of system calls, input/output operations, and API functions, giving you the insight to pinpoint and squash even the most cryptic bugs
