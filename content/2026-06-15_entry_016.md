**Imagining Loops**

Imagine a robot on a never-ending dance floor, executing the same funky moves over and over. That's basically what a "loop" is in programming: a repetitive instruction that continues to run until a specific condition is met, kind of like the robot's internal dance party clock. When the condition is fulfilled, the party stops, and the robot moves on to the next task – or sometimes, it even jumps back to the beginning to bust out those sweet moves again.
