Imagine a never-ending library where books represent lines of code. Each book has a unique title and author, but to navigate the shelves, you need a map that connects the dots between them – enter the concept of **linked lists**. A linked list is like a series of sticky notes tied together by a single thread, where each note holds a value and a pointer to the next note. As you move through the list, you're essentially following the thread, retrieving values and traversing the notes in a non-linear, yet connected way.
