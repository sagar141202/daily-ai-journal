**A Fragmented Dream**

Imagine a world where memories exist as wisps of smoke, tangling themselves into eerie ballerinos on the ceiling of a forgotten opera house. Every time a character remembers a piece of their past, it manifests as a fleeting dance, suspended in mid-air - a pirouette of nostalgia, a waltz of longing. As the performances unfold, they begin to warp and blur, revealing a hidden narrative thread that exposes the darkest secrets of the house, and the people who haunt it.
