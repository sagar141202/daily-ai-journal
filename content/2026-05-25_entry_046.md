**The Magic of Loops:**

Imagine being a robot tasked with folding a never-ending stack of laundry. Each fold, you repeat the same motion: grab, fold, replace. This repetitive action is a simple example of a **loop**, a programming concept that lets you execute a set of instructions over and over, saving you (or your robot friend) time and effort. With loops, you can automate tasks, create patterns, and even generate art – making coding an endless adventure!
