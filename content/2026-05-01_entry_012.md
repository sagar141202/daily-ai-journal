**Morning Momentum** 

Imagine bursting into your day like a sunrise, leaving chaos in the darkness. Starting each morning with a 10-minute "Stop-Start-Steal" session - stopping to review the previous day's accomplishments, starting a new task with an energized mindset, and stealing 10 minutes to tackle a nagging to-do - can propel you into a productivity-powered morning.

As the world outside awakens, begin each day with a **5-4-3-2-1** ritual: Five deep breaths to calm the mind, four moments to envision success, three gratitudes for existing blessings, two affirmations to boost confidence, and one clear purpose for tackling the day ahead.

**The 5-Minute Rule** - in a world that values time and efficiency, commit to dedicating the first five minutes of each day to planning and prioritizing tasks. This fleeting yet transformative act can set the tone for a productive, clutter-free, and purpose-driven day
