**The Midnight Oil**: For the die-hard Linux enthusiast who insists on sipping their coffee at 3 a.m., there's `watch` – a command that's as much about performance as it is about procrastination. By prefixing a command with `watch -n 2`, you can have your system execute it on a repeating loop every 2 seconds, effectively turning your terminal into a mesmerizing spectacle of scrolling output. It's like having a cat's video on repeat, but more functional.
