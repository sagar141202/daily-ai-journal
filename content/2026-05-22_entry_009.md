Imagine yourself floating on a cloud, gently swaying to the rhythm of the wind. To achieve such serenity, try "sensory separation." Before sleep, dedicate 10 minutes to silencing one sense at a time - earplugs for hearing, an eye mask for sight, and a gentle touch for feeling. By disengaging, your brain is more likely to enter the tranquil realm of dreams.
