**The Unsung Hero: `strace`**

Imagine a detective who follows the digital breadcrumbs of a program, unraveling the mysteries of how it interacts with the operating system. That's what `strace` does, providing a cryptic yet invaluable trail of system calls made by any process. By executing `strace -p <pid>`, you summon this digital sleuth to reveal the inner workings of your applications, helping you debug, troubleshoot, and optimize with unprecedented clarity.
