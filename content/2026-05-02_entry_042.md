**The Recipe of Recursion**

Imagine baking a cake that's made up of layers. Each layer needs to be baked, and once it's done, you place it on top of another, creating a tower of delicious treats. Recursion is like a recipe that says 'bake another layer, and then put it on top of the previous one.' This helps the program to accomplish a task that's similar, but slightly different each time, creating a never-ending tower of problem-solving possibilities.
