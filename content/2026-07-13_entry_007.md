**Stream-of-Consciousness**

The moment a task pops into our minds, our brains start racing, but to harness that energy, we must catch ourselves mid-sprint, pause, and jot down the thought - in an actual, tangible notebook - before our minds dissolve it like sand between our fingers. This simple act of record-keeping becomes the seed of productivity, a tangible thread to weave through our chaotic thoughts, keeping us grounded and focused. As we let the scribbles pile up, our minds begin to see patterns, and a symphony of to-do's starts to emerge, our once-fragmented thoughts dancing in harmony.

**Short and Sweet**

Use the '2-minute rule': if a task can be completed in less than two minutes, do it immediately. No excuses. Don't let it clutter your mental inbox. The seconds add up, and your time becomes your own.
