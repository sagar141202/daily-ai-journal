**Tip: Slicing Strings**

Unleash the hidden ninja within you by mastering Python's slicing feature! By using `my_string[start:stop:step]`, you can slice and dice strings like a hot knife through butter. For example, `hello[1:3]` will give you the substring `"el"`, while `hello[:3]` will give you `"hel"`. With this tip, you'll be string-jousting like a pro in no time!
