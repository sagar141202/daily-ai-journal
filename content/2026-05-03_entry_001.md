**Like a Canvas**

Imagine your mind as a canvas, once blank but now a jumbled mess of thoughts and worries. To achieve better sleep, try to 'wipe the canvas clean' each night by taking a few minutes to write down everything that's been swirling in your head. This simple act can calm your racing thoughts and allow your mind to surrender to the peaceful darkness.

**Like a Wave**

The ocean can be an intimidating force, but it's also a great teacher when it comes to sleep. Just as the tide ebbs and flows, our minds are constantly in motion, and if we don't learn to 'let go,' the stress can be overwhelming. Try practicing a gentle 'evasion' at the end of the day, letting go of the day's worries as you drift off to sleep.
