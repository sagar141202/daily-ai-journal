**The Pomodoro Pulse**
Imagine your mind as a hummingbird, flitting between flowers of focus and distraction. To harness its energy, try the technique of "Pomodoro intervals," where you work in short, 25-minute sprints, followed by a 5-minute break, to maintain a pace that's both productive and restorative. This simple habit can help you build sustained momentum and cultivate a mindset that's both driven and resilient.
