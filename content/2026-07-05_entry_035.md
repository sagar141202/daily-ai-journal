**Imagining Loops**
Imagine you're on a train hurtling towards a distant destination, and with each passing stop, you jump onto a new carriage, only to return to the previous one to pick up more passengers. This is essentially what a **while loop** does in programming - it continues to execute a block of code until a certain condition is met, much like your train ride keeps going until you reach your final destination.
