**In Motion, Not Momentum** 
Like a river flowing over smooth stones, our productivity builds momentum as we steadily add small, deliberate actions to our daily routine. By prioritizing '5 before 5', doing five quick, focused tasks before the end of each workday, we harness the power of forward motion – a gentle, unstoppable force that propels us toward our goals.

**The Power of Proximity** 
Imagine your tasks as physical objects stacked on a shelf, easily accessible when needed. When you group similar tasks, label them with color-coded stickers, and display them on a visual board, you tap into the productivity-boosting magic of proximity. This simple habit lets you quickly scan the space and tackle projects in the order that matters most.

**A Symphony of Small Wins** 
Envision your day as a harmonious composition of small tasks performed with precision and care. A series of tiny victories – responding to a crucial email, completing a report, or making a phone call
